The Taxi Listing Assistant Bookmarklet allows you to quickly copy the key elements of a Taxi listing and paste this info, pre-formatted, into a tab-separated document such as a Numbers or Excel file.

Navigate to the Taxi listings page: https://www.taximusic.com/listings.php, and then open the Listing Assistant bookmarklet by clicking on it from your browser's Bookmarket or Favorites bar, or from within your browser's bookmark manager. When the Taxi Listing Assistant bookmarklet is opn, you can then select with items Listing Assistant will search for by checking the checkboxes next to each option.Currently, the Taxi Listing Assistant allows you to capture: Listing ID, Title, Due Date. Description (first paragraph only), Queue Length, Keywords and the audio examples.

When you find a listing you are interested in capturing, just click on the title of the listing and a browser window will open up with all the items you want to capture. Copy what you see inside this window and click OK to close it.

Then open up Numbers or Excel and paste the items inside. Done!


* It's only been tested on Mac OS: Chrome, Safari and Firefox.
